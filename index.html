Culture
Before You Start
We are a fully remote team. Where you work doesn't matter — what matters is the quality and ownership of your work.
Expectation 1
Understand your responsibilities clearly
Expectation 2
Use your Role Hub and SOPs as your source of truth
Expectation 3
Communicate early if you're stuck or unsure
Expectation 4
Take ownership of outcomes — not just tasks
This isn't a place to complete tasks and clock out. We value people who care — those who take initiative, improve systems, and solve problems without waiting to be asked. If that sounds like you, you'll do well here. Let's get to work. 🚀
